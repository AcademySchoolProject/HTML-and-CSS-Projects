All About Pandas:

The current Panda population is around 1800 in their natural habitat.

Pandas are no longer endangered and are now considered Vulnerable!

Pandas do not hibernate, instead they just move down the mountain to warmer conditions.

Baby Pandas

Baby pandas are born pink, hairless and blind! Just like puppies! Their size is around the length of a pencil. When born they are very dependant on their mother, but at around 18 months its time for independancy!

Pink! Blind! Hairless! Independent!
Panda Facts

In China, the giant panda is a national treasure. They have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food. Due to their diet being 99% Vegetarian this means that they also eat other things like eggs, small animals, and carrion. Although they can eat that, they prefer everything bamboo. Another neat fact is that they don't hibernate!

Vulnerable! 99% Vegetarian! Loves Bamboo! Super Fluffy! Camouflage! Heavy Eaters!
Adult Pandas

Female pandas give birth to baby pandas every 2 years. Their pregnancies have a 50-60% chance to be twins! Adult pandas can live up to 30 years old in captivity. Pandas heavily rely on scent and tend to mark their territory with pee.

Black and White Cuddly! Cute! Heavy!
